So nice to meet you!

As a graphic designer, Maddison has come a long way since building Angelfire websites on her mom’s clunky Dell computer in 2003.

Using her skills in web design and digital illustration, Maddison refines and refreshes vintage style designs for the modern palette.

The tools she uses are the Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, the Google Suite, Maya, and can write in HTML, CSS, and javascript.

She has experimented with many different mediums including hand-drawn animations, motion graphics, print layouts, product design, branding and logo design, and Social Media content creation.

Currently studying at Santa Monica College pursuing a bachelor’s in Interaction Design, Maddison has a thriving freelance practice.
